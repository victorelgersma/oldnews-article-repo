**Notes on the OCR Cleanup:**
*   **Calculations:** In the telescope section, the OCR had "28}" and "power of OG". Based on the context of the subsequent math ($28.5 \times 12 = 342$ and the mention of 2304), I have corrected these to **$28\frac{1}{2}$** and **96**.
*   **The Big Number:** The remoteness of the cluster was rendered as a very long string. I have preserved the digits as they appeared, though they may represent a 19th-century calculation of miles.
*   **Ambiguous names:** "Laptace" was corrected to **Laplace**, "Lagraneg" to **Lagrange**, and "Mts" (where it referred to Herschel's observation) was corrected to **he**. "Nicwow" was corrected to **Nichol**.
*   **Scientific terms:** "eycular" was corrected to **circular**; "subordinae" to **subordinate**.
*   **Typos:** "insiguificance" corrected to **insignificance**, "enab!ed" to **enabled**, and "insufliciently" to **insufficiently**.
