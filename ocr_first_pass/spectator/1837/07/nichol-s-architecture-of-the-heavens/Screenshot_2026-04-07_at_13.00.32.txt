for example, “ may be made to revolve with sufficient rapidity to

say of caoutchouc, what would have resulted in such a case ?
Clearly, a separation of the rim from the mass of the rotatory body
—it would have expanded somewhat, just as the orbit of a planet
in a similar position; and, had other circumstances permitted,
