| ‘As to results which may flow from any particular view which
‘reason may show as the best supported, I must firmly protest against
any assumed title in an opponent to pronounce what these are, The
first object is to ascertain truth, No truth can be derogatory to the
presumed fountain of all truth. The derogation must lie in the erro-
neous construction which a weak human creature puts upon the
truth, And practically it is the true infidel state of mind which
prompts apprehension regarding any fact of nature, or any conclusion of
sound argument.”*
